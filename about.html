About Our School

Our History

Our Journey

Kiatine Nazarene Academy was founded in 2006 with the vision of providing quality, affordable, and Christian-based education to children in Mutonguni Ward and beyond. Since our humble beginnings, the school has steadily grown into a center of academic excellence, producing well-rounded learners who are prepared for both higher education and life skills.

Guided by Christian values, our mission has always been to nurture students’ intellectual, spiritual, and social growth, equipping them to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Learn More

Our Motto, Vision & Mission

Motto: We shine for the lord

Vision: Vision: Knowing every child's safety by inspiring, challenging, empowering, and changing the future of children living in poverty through education and spiritual well-being.

Mission: Mission: To provide quality education to children living in poverty through improved learning conditions, teacher training and development, and inclusive partnership with the community and county governments.

We are committed to excellence in teaching, innovation in learning, and molding learners to become self-reliant, responsible, and creative problem solvers.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our actions.
  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in academics, behavior, and service.
  • Faith: Our foundation is built on Christian teachings and values.
  • Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and ensure every child feels valued and supported.
  • Community: We foster a sense of belonging and collaboration among students, parents, and staff.

At Kiatine Nazarene Academy, we believe in shaping the future by investing in the holistic development of every child. Our commitment to nurturing young minds and hearts ensures that our students are equipped to face the challenges of tomorrow with confidence and resilience.
