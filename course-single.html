About CBC at Kiatine Nazarene Academy

The Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) is Kenya’s new education system that focuses on developing learners’ skills, values, and competencies. At Kiatine Nazarene Academy, we embrace CBC as a pathway to raise well-rounded learners who are prepared for the challenges of the 21st century.

Structure of CBC

  • Pre-Primary (PP1 & PP2): Early childhood learning focusing on literacy, numeracy, creativity, and social skills.
  • Lower Primary (Grade 1–3): Building foundational knowledge while nurturing talents and curiosity.
  • Upper Primary (Grade 4–6): Strengthening academic subjects, introducing practical skills, and developing responsibility.
  • Junior School (Grade 7–9): Preparing learners for career paths, entrepreneurship, and holistic growth.

Benefits of CBC

  • Encourages practical skills rather than rote learning.
  • Promotes creativity, innovation, and problem-solving.
  • Focuses on values, discipline, and social responsibility.
  • Allows learners to discover and nurture talents in sports, music, arts, and technology.

Our Commitment

At Kiatine Nazarene Academy, we integrate CBC with Christian values, ensuring that every child grows spiritually, morally, socially, and academically. Our teachers are CBC-trained, and we use interactive methods, technology, and extracurricular activities to make learning engaging and meaningful.
