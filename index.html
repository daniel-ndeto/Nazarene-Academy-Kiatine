About Our School

Kiatine Nazarene Academy is a Christian-based institution dedicated to providing Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) education. We combine academic excellence with spiritual, moral, and social growth to ensure every child reaches their full potential. Our supportive environment, qualified teachers, and modern facilities help learners shine both in academics and life.
